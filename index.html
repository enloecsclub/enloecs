2023-2024 Update

We are excited to announce that Enloe CS Club will be running again this year! Anyone who is interested in Computer Science, regardless of skill level, is welcome to join! If you are interested in joining and are not already on the email list, fill out this form: Enloe CS Interest and join our remind: @enloecs24, so we can send you any updates! We will be hosting our 2023-24 Interest Meeting on Tuesday 9/19, in Mr. Potter's Room (1202). We'll see you there!

About Us

Computer Science Club is a club whose goal is to create an inclusive environment of students passionate about computer science and develop their skills in programming, collaboration, and project management skills. Our plans for the upcoming school year are to have students participate in competitions such as Ready Set App and USACO, work on year-long projects, bring in guest speakers from tech companies in the RTP area, and so much more! Meetings will be held monthly, we hope to see you there! You can email us at enloe.cs.club@gmail.com.
